Pattern One
01

The Settlement
Black Hole

⚠ Trust Erosion

These are your lowest-risk customers — lost at the exact moment they were ready to buy their next product.

Customer Voice

Diagnosis & CRM Fix

Customers who complete their obligation get trapped in a post-settlement void — bank accounts frozen, no digital closure confirmation, no SIMAH update.

This is a potential automation gap between collections, SIMAH reporting, and closure confirmation.

"The customer who finishes paying is your best prospect for the next product. The settlement moment is a loyalty event — not a closing file."

Diagnosis & CRM Fix

The customer feels deceived — not because the product is dishonest, but because the onboarding journey fails to explain revolving credit mechanics in real time.

The CRM knows every transaction but sends no transparency signal. The customer discovers the impact only after accumulation.

Basita App Feature Suggestion

Add an interactive installment dashboard inside Basita that shows the customer in real time exactly how each withdrawal affects their monthly payment — before they confirm the transaction.

"Clarity is the foundation of loyalty. A customer who understands what they owe stays. A customer who discovers it later leaves — and tells ten people."
Pattern Three
03

Brand Suicide
During Collections

⚠ SAMA Reputation Risk
Customer Voice

"صار لي ظرف تأخرت صار جوالي كله رسائل تهديد من موظفينهم.. نصيحة لا تأخذون منهم."

ملتزمة سابقاً — Google Maps

"أخلاق الموظف سيئة جداً."

Google Maps Review

Diagnosis & CRM Fix

The collections system treats all customers equally — a first-time delay gets the same aggressive response as a repeat defaulter. This destroys years of loyalty in one interaction.

Root cause: absence of customer segmentation in the CRM. Without loyalty tiers, a High-Value customer looks identical to a risk case. This also carries SAMA compliance risk — aggressive collections behavior is under increasing regulatory scrutiny.

"Retaining a loyal customer costs 5x less than acquiring a new one. The collections script that loses a loyal customer is the most expensive message Tasheel ever sends — to the customer, and to the regulator."
The CRM Solution

Three Fixes.
One Framework.

Each solution is measurable, scalable, and policy-driven.

01

Closure Journey Automation

Upon final payment: instant digital settlement certificate + automated SIMAH update + personalized re-engagement offer. The settlement moment becomes a retention trigger.

KPI: Settlement-to-offer conversion rate
02

Real-Time Transparency Engine

Every credit utilization triggers an instant CRM notification: "Your new installment will be X for Y months." The customer sees impact within 60 seconds — not at month end.

KPI: Billing complaint recurrence rate
03

Loyalty-Informed Collections

CRM segments customers by loyalty tier. High-tier customers with clean history receive support-first messaging on first delay. Escalation only after tier-appropriate grace window.

KPI: Loyal customer churn rate post-delay
"These aren't three separate fixes — they're one CRM governance framework that makes every customer touchpoint intentional."

→
