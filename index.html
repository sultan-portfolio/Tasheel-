What I Did

Before this conversation, I analyzed Tasheel Finance's public customer feedback across Google Maps and app reviews — looking for patterns, not complaints. Three systemic patterns emerged.

The Frame

Every pattern here is not a failure — it's a missed loyalty moment. Each one represents customers who wanted to stay loyal but weren't given the infrastructure to do so.

The Lens

CRM & Loyalty is not a marketing tool. It's a governance engine — one that turns operational touchpoints into measurable trust, retention, and LTV growth.

The Goal

Transform Tasheel's CRM from a reactive collections tracker into a proactive loyalty growth system — where every interaction either builds or protects customer lifetime value.

The Problem

The CRM knows every transaction but sends no transparency signal. The customer discovers the financial impact only after months of accumulation.

The onboarding journey fails to explain revolving credit mechanics — leaving customers to discover the rules after the damage is done.

The CRM Fix
1
Real-Time NotificationsEvery credit utilization triggers instant CRM push: "Your new installment will be X for Y months." Within 60 seconds, not end of month.
Basita App Feature

Add an interactive dashboard showing how each withdrawal affects the monthly payment — before the customer confirms.

The Problem

The collections system treats all customers equally — a first-time delay from a loyal customer gets the same aggressive response as a chronic defaulter.

Root cause: no customer segmentation in the CRM. Without loyalty tiers informing collections behavior, years of trust are destroyed in one SMS.

The CRM Fix
1
Loyalty-Informed CollectionsHigh-tier customers receive support-first messaging on first delay. Escalation only after tier-appropriate grace window.
2
Supportive CollectionsReplace threat messages with: "We noticed a delay — can we help?" First contact is empathy, not enforcement.
★
KPILoyal customer churn rate post-delay
"Retaining a loyal customer costs 5x less than acquiring a new one. The collections script that loses a loyal customer is the most expensive message Tasheel ever sends — to the customer, and to the regulator."
The CRM Solution

Three Fixes. One Framework.

Each solution is measurable, scalable, and policy-driven.

01

Closure Journey Automation

Final payment → instant settlement certificate + SIMAH update + personalized re-engagement offer. Settlement becomes a retention trigger.

KPI: Settlement-to-offer conversion
02

Real-Time Transparency Engine

Every credit utilization → instant CRM notification with new installment amount. Customer sees impact within 60 seconds, not end of month.

KPI: Billing complaint recurrence
03

Loyalty-Informed Collections

CRM segments by loyalty tier. High-tier customers get support-first messaging on first delay. Escalation follows tier-appropriate grace window.

KPI: Loyal churn rate post-delay
"These aren't three separate fixes — they're one CRM governance framework that makes every customer touchpoint intentional."
