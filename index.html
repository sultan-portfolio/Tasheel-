Context & Methodology

The Opportunity Hidden in Plain Sight

This document is not a critique—it is a map of untapped potential.

What I Did

Before our conversation, I analyzed Tasheel Finance's public customer feedback across Google Maps and app reviews. I wasn't looking for complaints; I was looking for systemic patterns. These aren't just bugs—they are gaps where loyal customers are falling through the cracks.

The Framework

Every pattern discussed here is a missed loyalty moment. These are customers who wanted to remain loyal but encountered friction that eroded their trust. My goal is to transform Tasheel's CRM from a reactive tracker into a proactive Loyalty Governance Engine.

The Objective

To move Tasheel's strategy from "Collection-Centric" to "Relationship-Centric," ensuring that every touchpoint—especially the difficult ones like settlement and debt—either builds or protects Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).

The Outcome

A 90-day plan focused on three "Levers": Automation of closure, Transparency of credit usage, and Loyalty-informed collections. These are the pillars of the eXtra Advantage—leveraging your retail ecosystem for dominance.

Pattern One
⚠ Trust Erosion
The Settlement Black Hole

Losing low-risk customers at the exact moment they were ready to buy their next product.

01
Customer Voice

"I already 2 month payment was done but nothing from their side... suddenly call today saying that i need to pay all the amount."

Google Maps Review

"سددت أول بأول لمدة 24 شهر وخلصت.. لي 5 أشهر أتواصل معهم أقولهم خلصت، ليه ما سويتوا مخالصة؟"

Google Maps Review

The Problem

Customers who complete their obligation get trapped in a post-settlement void. No digital closure confirmation, and worse, no SIMAH update.

This automation gap turns your best prospects into angry detractors exactly when they're ready for their next product.

The CRM Fix
1
Instant Closure Journey: Final payment triggers: digital settlement certificate + automated SIMAH update within 24hrs.
2
Next-Best-Offer (NBO): Automated re-engagement 7 days post-settlement. The settlement becomes a sales trigger.
"The settlement moment is a loyalty event — not a closing file."
Pattern Two
⚠ Clarity Breakdown
The Transparency Gap

Uninformed customers feel deceived. Clarity is the foundation of loyalty.

02
Customer Voice

"كل ما استخدم رصيد بسيطة يصير لي أقساط زيادة.. موظف بسيطة ما وضح لي."

App Review

"I took 3600 from them, and now they want me to pay 6820."

Google Review

The Problem

The CRM knows the math, but the customer doesn't. Onboarding fails to explain revolving credit mechanics clearly.

Customers discover the "cost of credit" too late, leading to "billing shock" and high churn rates in the Basita product.

The CRM Fix
Basita App Feature Proposal

Impact Dashboard: In-app dashboard showing "Effect on Monthly Installment" before the customer confirms a withdrawal.

✔
Automated Education: 3-part educational sequence explaining "How Basita works" in the first 48 hours of activation.
Pattern Three
⚠ SAMA Reputation Risk
Brand Suicide During Collections

Aggressive collections destroy brand equity and increase regulatory risk.

03
Customer Voice

"صار لي ظرف تأخرت صار جوالي كله رسائل تهديد.. نصيحة لا تأخذون منهم."

"أخلاق الموظف سيئة جداً في المتابعة.. الموظفين يفتقدون للحد الأدنى من التعامل."

The Problem

The collections system treats all customers equally — no customer segmentation in the CRM informing collections behavior.

Aggressive scripts for first-time delays from previously loyal customers lead to permanent brand damage.

The CRM Fix
★
Empathy-First Collections: High-tier loyalists get "Support-First" messaging & grace windows instead of penalties.
✔
Regulatory Alignment: Aligning collections tone with SAMA's principles of fair treatment.
