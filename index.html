Customer Pain Points

Identified three critical friction points that reduce repeat engagement and loyalty.

Example: Delayed responses, inconsistent service, unclear rewards.

Insight #1 — Response Time

Fast, proactive responses increase satisfaction and conversion rates by up to 35%.

Insight #2 — Consistency

Every touchpoint must align with brand promise; inconsistency erodes trust.

Insight #3 — Rewards Transparency

Loyalty programs must be intuitive and easily trackable by customers.

CRM Implementation

Introducing a centralized CRM system to track all interactions, automate follow-ups, and measure engagement.

Loyalty Framework

Tiered rewards, personalized offers, and predictive analytics to drive repeat engagement.

Expected Outcomes

Increase retention, satisfaction, and revenue — measurable via CRM dashboards.

Next Steps

Deploy CRM, integrate loyalty program, train staff, monitor metrics, iterate.
