Context & Methodology

The Opportunity Hidden in Plain Sight

This document is not a critique—it is a map of untapped potential.

What I Did

Before our conversation, I analyzed Tasheel Finance's public customer feedback across Google Maps and app reviews. I wasn't looking for complaints; I was looking for systemic patterns. These aren't just technical bugs—they are psychological gaps where loyal customers are falling through the cracks and losing trust in the brand promise.

The Framework

Every pattern discussed here represents a missed loyalty moment. These are customers who wanted to remain loyal but encountered friction that eroded their trust. My goal is to transform Tasheel's CRM from a reactive tracking tool into a proactive Loyalty Governance Engine that anticipates needs.

The Objective

To move Tasheel's strategy from "Collection-Centric" to "Relationship-Centric," ensuring that every touchpoint—especially the difficult ones like settlement and debt—either builds or protects Customer Lifetime Value (LTV). We want customers for life, not just for one contract.

The Outcome

A 90-day plan focused on three "Levers": Automation of closure, Transparency of credit usage, and Loyalty-informed collections. These are the pillars of the eXtra Advantage—leveraging your retail ecosystem for total market dominance.

Analysis Pattern One
⚠ Trust Erosion
The Settlement Black Hole

Losing low-risk customers at the exact moment they were ready to buy their next product.

01
Customer Voice

"I already 2 month payment was done but nothing from their side... suddenly call today saying that i need to pay all the amount. Worst finance."

Google Maps Review

"سددت أول بأول لمدة 24 شهر وخلصت.. لي 5 أشهر أتواصل معهم أقولهم خلصت، ليه ما سويتوا مخالصة؟"

Google Maps Review

The Problem

Customers who complete their obligation get trapped in a post-settlement void. There is no digital closure confirmation, and worse, no SIMAH update.

This automation gap turns your highest-quality prospects into angry detractors at the precise moment they are most eligible for a new product.

The CRM Fix
1
Instant Closure Journey: Final payment automatically triggers a digital settlement certificate and SIMAH update within 24hrs.
2
Next-Best-Offer (NBO): Automated re-engagement 7 days post-settlement. The settlement becomes a sales trigger, not a dead end.
"The settlement moment is a loyalty event — not just a closing file."
Analysis Pattern Two
⚠ Clarity Breakdown
The "Basita" Transparency Gap

Uninformed customers feel deceived. Clarity is the bedrock of long-term loyalty.

02
Customer Voice

"كل ما استخدم رصيد بسيطة يصير لي أقساط زيادة.. موظف بسيطة ما وضح لي شي."

App Store Review

"I took 3600 from them, and now they want me to pay 6820. Be careful."

Google Maps Review

The Problem

The CRM knows the math, but the customer doesn't. Onboarding fails to explain revolving credit mechanics clearly to the average user.

Customers discover the "cost of credit" too late, leading to "billing shock," resentment, and eventual app deletion.

The CRM Fix
Basita Feature Proposal

Impact Dashboard: In-app visual showing exactly how much the "New Monthly Total" will be before the customer confirms a withdrawal.

✔
Automated Education: A 3-part CRM sequence explaining "How Basita works" in plain Arabic/English within the first 48 hours of activation.
Analysis Pattern Three
⚠ SAMA Reputation Risk
Collections Tone & Segmentation

Aggressive collections destroy years of brand building in a single phone call.

03
Customer Voice

"صار لي ظرف تأخرت صار جوالي كله رسائل تهديد.. نصيحة لا تأخذون منهم."

"أخلاق الموظف سيئة جداً في المتابعة.. الموظفين يفتقدون للحد الأدنى من التعامل الراقي."

The Problem

The collections system treats all customers equally. There is no segmentation in the CRM informing collections behavior based on past loyalty.

Aggressive scripts for first-time delays from previously perfect payers lead to permanent brand damage and regulatory complaints.

The CRM Fix
★
Empathy-First Collections: High-tier loyalists get "Support-First" messaging and grace windows instead of immediate penalties.
✔
Regulatory Guardrails: Aligning collections tone with SAMA's fair-treatment principles through automated script gating.
